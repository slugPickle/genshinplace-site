Enkanomiya (Japanese: 淵下宮 "Palace Below the Depths"), originally known as Byakuyakoku (Japanese: 白夜国 "Land of the White Night / Midnight Sun") and Tokoyokoku (Japanese: 常世国 "Eternal Country"), is a location under Inazuma. It can be entered via a deep pool east of Sangonomiya Shrine, and legend has it that the Watatsumi people originated from here. Although Enkanomiya is located under Watatsumi Island, it is still part of Teyvat, though somewhat disconnected from it.

To enter Enkanomiya, the seals on the five Goshou Rocks need to be broken for the pearls located within the shrines for the Key of the Moon-bathed Deep, which would allow entry to Enkanomiya, provided that the spirit veins connecting Enkanomiya and Watatsumi Island are flowing smoothly and a person whose heart is "as clear as water". According to legend, the key was forged from Orobashi's blood.
